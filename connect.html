about >>

Workaholic, overachiever, Type A, no-nonsense, quick action... These used to describe me.

I used to take life so seriously that it had become such a "task". I was pragmatic and efficient "working" life to its best. Well, at least that's what I thought, until one day I realized what I've missed in life and learned the true meaning of time (and life)...

I used to think that time was precious, so I was always in a hurry to do things to make good use of time.
Then for a while, time was meaningless to me because I couldn't turn back the clock.
Now I see the preciousness and essence of time, so I take my time to do things, that is, to live.
Take the time for yourself and people you love. Explore this world you live in to the fullest.
Appreciate, enjoy and love life. It's not worth the "efficiency" when you realize what is actually sacrificed.
Be present, be still, be content, be your true self.

I see this website as my platform to share writings, activities, visuals, and projects as a channel to communicate the meaning of Life - to understand & fulfill our Purpose, to embrace Love, and to experience Joy fearlessly. I would also like to mentor others who are interested in finding the answers to the “big question” of what life is all about, and how to live it to the fullest potential, please come JOURNEY WITH ME!
